Our Product

BNCC Learning and Training

LnT is a software training facility that will be provided specifically to BNCC members to develop their technical skills with a variety of materials tailored to future needs in the world of work. This LnT is held at the BNCC Secretariat regularly (once a week)

The material is:


Java Programming.
Web Design.
Web Programming.
3D Modeling and Animation.
Mobile Applications.

And of course with facilities that really support the learning atmosphere of its members, such as:


Competent teachers in their fields.
LnT room with full AC.
One computer is available per member.


BNCC will also provide graduation certificates at the end of the LnT academic year, which of course will add to the portfolio of BNCC members so that it will help in preparation for the world of work later.
