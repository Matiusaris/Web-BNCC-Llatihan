Our Product

FileMagz

Filemagz is an online media that discusses technology trends, the lives of young people, and also business trends such as startups that are developing in Indonesia and the world. Filemagz aspires to always publish various kinds of content that is interesting, informative and useful for society, especially for young people in Indonesia.

Previously, Filemagz was a free magazine with an IT Lifestyle concept, first published on December 19 2007. Previously, Filemagz was a print magazine that was published bi-monthly and distributed to a number of pick-up points that had collaborated with Filemagz. As time went by, the internet began to develop. The internet has become a lifestyle and is difficult to remove from one's life.
